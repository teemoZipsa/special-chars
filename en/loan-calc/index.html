📖 Repayment Method Guide

Fixed Payment (Amortized) — Pay the same total amount (principal + interest) each month. Easy to plan, but total interest is the highest.

Fixed Principal — Pay a fixed principal amount plus decreasing interest each month. Higher initial payments, but lower total interest.

Interest Only (Bullet) — Pay only interest each month and repay the entire principal at maturity. Total interest is the highest.


💡 Under the same conditions, Fixed Principal results in the lowest total interest.

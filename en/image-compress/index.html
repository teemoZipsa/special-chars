💡 Comprehensive Guide to Image Compression

1. How do I compress images in my browser?

Our image compressor works entirely within your web browser. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Drag and drop your image (JPG, PNG, WebP) into the drop zone.
  2. Adjust the Quality slider. Lowering the quality reduces the file size more significantly.
  3. Set a Max Width to resize large photos automatically.
  4. Compare the original and compressed sizes in real-time, then click Download.
2. Is my picture data safe? Do you upload it?

Yes, your privacy is 100% guaranteed. Because our tool uses local browser processing, your images are never uploaded to any server. All compression and resizing happens directly on your own device, making it safe for sensitive or private photos.

3. Why compress images?

Reducing image file size is essential for web performance and storage management. Lighter images load faster on websites (boosting SEO), consume less mobile data, and make it easier to attach photos to emails or upload them to social media platforms that have file size limits.

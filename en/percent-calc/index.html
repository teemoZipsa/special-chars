💡 Percentage Calculator FAQ

📖 How to Use the 4 Modes
  • X% of Y: Calculate a specific percentage of a value (e.g., finding the tip or tax amount).
  • Change (%): Find the percentage increase or decrease between two numbers (e.g., measuring price changes or growth).
  • Ratio: Determine what percentage a part is of a whole (e.g., calculating test scores or project progress).
  • Reverse: Find the original value before a percentage was added or subtracted (e.g., finding the pre-discount price).
✂️ How do I use the quick input buttons?

We've provided quick-select buttons for common percentages (5%, 10%, 15%, etc.) and operations (Discount vs. Markup) to speed up your calculations. In "X% of Y" mode, just click a button to instantly apply that percentage.

📋 How do I copy the results?

Simply click anywhere inside the result box. The calculated value will be automatically copied to your clipboard without any formatting, ready to paste.

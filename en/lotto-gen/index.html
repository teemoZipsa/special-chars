💡 Comprehensive Guide to Generating Lotto Numbers

1. How do I generate my lucky lotto numbers?

Using our lottery number generator is very straightforward:

  • Generate: Click the "Generate Numbers" button to instantly pick 6 completely random, unique numbers from 1 to 45.
  • Include (Blue): Click a number once in the options grid. This number will be guaranteed to appear in your final combination (You can include up to 5 numbers).
  • Exclude (Red): Click a number twice. This number gives you total control by being explicitly excluded from the drawing pool.
2. Are these random lottery numbers truly random?

Yes. We use a cryptographically strong mathematical formula (the Fisher-Yates shuffle algorithm) directly in your browser. This ensures that every possible combination of lottery numbers has an equal, mathematically fair chance of being generated.

3. What do the different ball colors mean?

We use standard Korean 6/45 lottery colors for better visualization:

  • Yellow: Numbers 1 to 10
  • Blue: Numbers 11 to 20
  • Red: Numbers 21 to 30
  • Gray: Numbers 31 to 40
  • Green: Numbers 41 to 45

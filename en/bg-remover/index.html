💡 Comprehensive Guide to AI Background Remover

1. Is this truly free and unlimited?

Yes, absolutely. Since the AI processing runs entirely on your device (using WebAssembly), there are no server costs on our end. This means you can use the tool as many times as you like, completely free.

2. Are my images safe?

100% Secure. Your files are never uploaded to the cloud or saved on any external servers. The Web AI model runs strictly within your browser, ensuring maximum privacy for your sensitive or personal photos.

3. Why is the AI model 40MB?

To deliver high-quality, professional edge detection without relying on a remote API, your browser securely downloads a compressed neural network model on your first visit. Once downloaded, your browser caches it, so subsequent uses are blazing fast even offline!

💡 Zodiac Calculator FAQ

📖 How to Use
  1. Select your date of birth or click the 'Today' button.
  2. Your zodiac sign and detailed information will be shown automatically.
  3. Use the 'Copy Result' button to easily share or save your reading.

💡 Zodiac signs are based on the period the Sun transits each constellation. If you were born on a cusp date, your sign may vary slightly depending on the exact time and year of your birth.

⭐ What is a Zodiac Sign?

Your zodiac sign (or sun sign) represents the position of the sun during your birth. In Western astrology, it forms the foundation of your core personality, ego, and basic preferences.

🌍 What do Elements and Planets mean?

Each sign belongs to one of four elements (Fire, Earth, Air, Water) which describe underlying temperaments, and is ruled by a planet that dictates its core motivations and characteristics.

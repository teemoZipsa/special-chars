💡 Wage Converter Guide & FAQ

📖 How to Use
  1. Enter any one of: hourly rate, monthly salary, or annual salary — the others auto-calculate.
  2. Adjust hours per day and days per week for accurate conversion.
  3. Toggle the tax deduction type to see your take-home (net) pay.
  4. Use the "How many hours" feature to see how long you'd need to work for any purchase.
⏰ How is overtime pay calculated?

Overtime hours (beyond the standard 8-hour workday) are typically compensated at 1.5x the regular hourly rate. Night-shift hours (10pm–6am) receive an additional 0.5x premium. These multipliers are applied automatically in this calculator.

📅 What is the Weekly Allowance?

In some labor systems, workers who work 15+ hours per week receive an additional paid day off equivalent. Toggle this on or off depending on your employment terms.

🧾 How does the tax deduction work?

Social Insurance (~9.4%) covers pension, health, long-term care, and employment insurance. Freelancer Tax (3.3%) is a simplified withholding for independent contractors. Your actual tax rate may vary based on your jurisdiction and circumstances.

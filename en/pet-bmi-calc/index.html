❓ FAQ
Why is standard weight important?
Obesity increases the risk of joint disease, diabetes, and heart problems. Aim to stay within 10-15% of ideal weight.

Can't we just use BMI like humans?
Due to large body type variations between breeds, BCS (Body Condition Score) + breed-specific standard weight is more accurate for pets.

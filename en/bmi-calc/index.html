📖 What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple measure that uses your height and weight to estimate your total body fat.

Formula: BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ [Height (m)]²


⚠️ Note: BMI does not account for muscle mass, bone density, age, or gender. It should be used as a general screening tool, not a diagnostic one.

💡 The ranges above use the standard WHO (World Health Organization) criteria for adults.

📖 Calculation Method
RER (Resting Energy Requirement)
RER = 70 × weight(kg)^0.75

DER (Daily Energy Requirement)
DER = RER × activity factor
• Puppy: ×2.0-3.0 / Adult: ×1.0-1.8 / Senior: ×1.0-1.4
• Reduce 10-15% if spayed/neutered

Food Amount Conversion
Calculated based on typical dry food at 3,500-4,000 kcal/kg.

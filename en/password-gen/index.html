💡 Comprehensive Guide to Secure Passwords

1. Is this password generator safe to use?

Absolutely. Our local password generator uses your browser's native cryptographic randomized math functions (crypto.getRandomValues). Your passwords are generated 100% locally on your device and are never sent across the internet or saved to any database. Once you close the tab, the history is gone forever.

2. What makes a strong password?

A truly secure password relies heavily on two factors: length and variety.

  • Length: We recommend a minimum of 12 to 16 characters. Every additional character makes it exponentially harder for a computer to crack.
  • Variety: You should check all the boxes: Uppercase, Lowercase, Numbers, and Symbols. This massively increases the pool of possible character combinations.
3. Can I recover a password if I forget to copy it?

Yes, as long as you haven't closed the window! Your 10 most recently generated random passwords are kept in the 'Recent History' section below. You can simply click on any past password in the list to instantly copy it to your clipboard.

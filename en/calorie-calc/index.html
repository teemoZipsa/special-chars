💡 FAQ
Q. What is BMR?

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the amount of energy you expend while at rest. Even if you sleep all day, your body burns this amount of calories just to keep your heart beating and lungs breathing. We use the highly accurate Mifflin-St Jeor equation.

Q. How many calories should I cut to lose weight?

A 500kcal daily deficit from your TDEE will usually result in about 0.5kg (1 lb) of weight loss per week. We do not recommend dropping below 1,500kcal for men or 1,200kcal for women, as drastic calorie reduction can cause muscle loss and metabolic slowdown.


Q. How are macro ratios calculated?

For Maintenance: Carbs 50% / Protein 30% / Fat 20%. For Weight Loss: Carbs 40% / Protein 40% / Fat 20% (higher protein prevents muscle loss). For Weight Gain: Carbs 50% / Protein 30% / Fat 20%.

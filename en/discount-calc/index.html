💡 Comprehensive Guide to Discount & Tax Calculator

1. How do I calculate a simple discount?

Enter your Original Price, then enter either the Discount Rate (%) or the specific Discount Amount ($). The calculator will automatically sync the two fields and immediately show your final discounted price below.

2. How does the Sales Tax feature work?

By toggling "Include Sales Tax (10%)", the calculator will compute a 10% tax on the after-discount amount and add it to your final total. This is crucial for accurate invoicing or shopping in regions with value-added taxes (VAT).

3. What is the "Input price is pre-tax" toggle used for?

If you have a price that already includes tax, but you need to know the base price and the tax amount separately (reverse calculation), turn this toggle off. The calculator will extract the tax value out of the given total for you.

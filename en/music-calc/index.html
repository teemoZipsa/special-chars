💡 How to Use

• BPM Tap: Tap the screen to the rhythm to measure the average BPM. Accuracy improves after 4+ taps.

• Metronome: Set a BPM and press play to hear precise click sounds. The first beat (downbeat) is accented with a higher pitch.

• Delay Timing: Get the delay/reverb sync times for your DAW. Shows ms values for each note subdivision.

• Song Length: Calculate total duration from bars and BPM, or reverse-calculate how many bars fit a given duration.

• Note·Hz: Convert between note names and frequencies. Adjust the A4 reference for custom tuning.

• Transpose: Shift the key by semitones and see the transposed scale. Also calculates BPM change for tape-style pitch shifting.
